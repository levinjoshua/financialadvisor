ICEA LION WHOLE OF LIFE PLAN

What is the ICEA LION Whole of Life Plan?

The ICEA LION Whole of Life Plan is a comprehensive protection plan designed to provide financial security by paying a lump sum amount of your choice. This plan ensures the following:


Protection for Your Loved Ones: In the unfortunate event of your passing, the plan offers financial security to your family, safeguarding them against potential loss of income and wealth. This prevents them from having to sell assets to make ends meet.


Coverage for You: The plan also provides financial support in case you suffer from a disability or a specified illness.


Additionally, the Whole of Life Plan serves as a valuable asset, similar to land, property, or stocks. It can be passed on to your loved ones as part of your estate, ensuring that the lump sum is paid directly to your beneficiaries.

How does it work?

With the ICEA LION Whole of Life Plan, you simply select the amount you wish to cover your loved ones.


You then pay a regular premium of your choice over a period of 10, 15, or 20 years.


Coverage continues for the rest of your life and pays the lump sum amount to your beneficiaries eventually

What are other features and benefits?

Tax benefit: You receive a tax relief of 15% of the premium, up to a maximum of KES 60,000 annually. This reduces your tax liability, which would otherwise be incurred without life insurance.


Flexible Premium Payments: Premiums start as low as KES 1,000 per month and can be paid on a monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual basis.


No Medical Examination: For lump sum amounts of up to KES 5 million, no medical examination is required.

Optional Benefits

By paying an additional premium, ICEA LION Life offers protection against the following:


1. Serious/Critical illness: This benefit provides a lump sum amount upon the first diagnosis of various serious conditions such as cancer, kidney failure, heart attack, surgery, stroke, paralysis, blindness, major organ transplant, multiple sclerosis, parkinson's, alzheimer's, arthritis, coma, burns, muscular dystrophy and more.

2. Permanent Disability: This benefit pays a lump sum amount on the first diagnosis of permanent total or partial disability, such as the loss of sight, hearing, limbs, or paralysis. The disability may arise from an accident or illness.


NOTE: The lump sum payment can help you seek treatment locally or abroad and assist with the costs involved in adjusting to changes in your daily living.
